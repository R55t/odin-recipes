Tomato Salsa Recipe

Make this homemade salsa anytime with ripe tomatoes, onion, jalapeños, and a few other fresh ingredients. It's very popular. Serve with tortilla chips.

Ingredients

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh caliantro
  • 1 tomatillo, diced (optional)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • salt to taste
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, minced

Directions

  1. Step 1Gather all Ingridients
  2. Step 2Combine tomatoes, onion, tomatillo, cilantro, garlic, lime juice, and salt in a medium-sized mixing bowl. Mix well.
  3. Step 3Add 1/2 of the jalapeño pepper and taste. If you desire your salsa with more kick, add remaining 1/2 jalapeño.
  4. Step 4Cover the salsa and chill until ready to serve.
